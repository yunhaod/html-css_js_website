Introduction

The Amazonia Hackathon aims to combat deforestation through hardware development. The hackathon took course over 2 days, with workshops, and panels hosted by Seeed Studio, Cadence Design Systems, and Nordic Semiconductor.

The Challenge

How can we design IoT device for Lib√©lula to combat deforestation through hardware innovation?

Outcome

Successfully pitched our project plan to the judges and won second place out of a pool consisting of over 100 participants around the world

Our Solution

In order to detect early stages of deforestation, we believe a change in animal movement pattern would be a key indication. To utilize that, our project consisted of GPS modules on animals and trees. We also planned to detect CO2 level spikes from chainsaw usage. The data transmitted from the sensors and GPS would undergo data cleaning and organization to produce the most appropriate notification.

Our Project Submission consisted of:

  • Google Doc detailing our research and project planning
  • Google Slide explaining our project and pitching to judges
  • Scala code for data set creation and cleaning
