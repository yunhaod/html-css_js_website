Overview

This project aimed to create a humidifier that is compatible with the Google ecosystem through the use of the Matter protocol. The humidifier is controllable through Google Assistant on the Google Nest Hub and the Google Home app.

Final Device Design

Outcome

The humidifier is able to react to commands from the Google Nest Hub and Google Assistant.

It can also be controlled through the Google Home app .

Hardware

An ESP32 was used interface with the Matter protocol as well as the water atomizer.

The physical model has space to store the electronics as well as an insert-able water tank at the back of the model.

Takeaways

Along each step in this project, it was fascinating to use one of the most newest IoT protocol to be incorporating into a personal design. This project definitely taught me more ways of testing, validating, debugging, and troubleshooting complicated systems.
